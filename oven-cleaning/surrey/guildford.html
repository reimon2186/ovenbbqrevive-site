Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Coordination

Your oven refresh begins the moment you get in touch for a free, no-obligation quote based on your appliance and its condition. Once you are happy with the estimate, we arrange an appointment that suits your schedule. On the day, we give you a quick call before we arrive to keep everything running smoothly.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

When we arrive, our technician carries out a full inspection of your oven and removes all detachable parts such as racks, and doors. These are placed into our heated dip tank to break down heavy grease and burnt-on residue while we prepare the main oven for cleaning.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

With the removable parts soaking, we focus on the interior of the oven. This includes all internal surfaces. We safely remove grease, carbon build-up, and stubborn deposits to restore the oven’s efficiency and appearance.

Step 4: Polishing & Reassembly

Once everything is fully cleaned, we rinse and polish the components removed earlier and carefully reassemble your oven. We ensure each part is fitted securely and looks its best.

Step 5: Final Touches

To finish, we carry out a final check, wipe down the surrounding area, and leave your oven looking fresh, hygienic, and ready to use. Our goal is to give you a spotless result with no mess left behind.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does a typical clean take?

    Most ovens take around one to two hours, depending on size and how much residue has built up. Range ovens can take up to four hours.

  • Do I need to empty the kitchen before you arrive?

    No. As long as we can get to the oven easily, we handle the rest. We will also leave our working area clean and tidy after we are done.

  • Will there be any strong smells afterwards?

    No. The products we use inside the home are low-odour and safe once the clean is complete. All chemicals used for cleaning will be neutralised prior to re-entering the house.
